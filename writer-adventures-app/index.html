Writer's Adventure

It's fun and easy to start a writer's adventure. The goal is to get you to write and be more creative when you get stuck or have blocks. Simply press the button to pick a line from a randomly generated poem. Once you press the button, a line will appear or you will be prompted to pick a line from the poem below. After you pick your line, include it anyway you want in a poem you write in the space provided below. Poems are not posted or saved, so please remember to save your work in a safe space, just for you!

Happy Writing!
